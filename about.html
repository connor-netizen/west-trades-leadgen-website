About West Trades Group

Built by people who understand construction, enhanced by technology that makes quality trade work accessible

Who We Are

West Trades Group was founded by professionals with extensive experience in commercial construction and modern technology. We've seen firsthand what makes projects succeed—quality trades, clear communication, and efficient coordination.

Based in West Auckland with deep roots in the Waitākere area, we've built a network of skilled, independent trade professionals across Auckland who share our commitment to quality work and professional service.

Our Approach

We believe great trade work shouldn't be complicated to access. By using modern coordination systems and smart project routing, we connect property owners with quality trade professionals more efficiently than traditional methods.

Every trade in our network is an independent operator—experienced professionals who take pride in their craft. We don't employ them; we partner with them to deliver better results for everyone.

Technology That Works

We use smart automation to handle the repetitive tasks—matching projects with trades, coordinating schedules, and managing communication—while personal oversight ensures quality and reliability. It's not about replacing people; it's about making everyone more effective.

Our Technology Philosophy

Modern tools that enhance work, not replace it

We use technology to coordinate projects more efficiently, but we're not a "tech platform" selling software. We're construction people using smart systems to deliver better service.

Built for Reliability

Our coordination approach operates on three levels to ensure consistent service:

Manual

Traditional methods ensure the business runs even if all technology fails.

Digital

Modern online systems create efficiency without complex dependencies.

Smart Tools

Automation optimizes matching and communication when available.

This approach means projects move smoothly with modern efficiency, while maintaining the reliability of proven traditional methods.
