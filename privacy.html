Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025 (Draft)

West Trades Group Ltd ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 2020.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our service, we collect the following information:

Information You Provide:

  • Contact Details: Name, email address, phone number
  • Project Information: Project description, location, budget, timeline, property type
  • Additional Details: Any other information you choose to provide in forms or communications

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Website Usage: Pages visited, time spent on site, browser type, device type
  • Analytics Data: Collected via Google Analytics (see section below)
  • IP Address: For security and analytics purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Connect you with relevant trade professionals from our network
  • Communicate with you about your project
  • Improve our service and website
  • Send occasional updates about our services (with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Analyze website performance and user behavior

3. Sharing Your Information

With Trade Professionals: We share your project details and contact information with relevant trade professionals in our network so they can provide quotes and services.

We Do Not:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your information for marketing purposes without consent
  • Provide your information to anyone not directly involved in your project

Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics collects information anonymously and reports website trends without identifying individual visitors.

Google Analytics uses cookies to track your interaction with our website. This information helps us improve user experience and understand which content is most valuable to visitors.

You can opt-out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Data Storage and Security

We store your information securely using industry-standard practices:

  • Form submissions are stored on secure servers via Netlify
  • Email communications are protected by secure email services
  • Access to personal information is limited to authorized personnel
  • We regularly review our security practices

Data Retention: We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion of your data at any time (see Your Rights below).

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies for:

  • Essential Functions: Form submissions and basic site functionality
  • Analytics: Google Analytics tracking (as described above)

Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer. This may prevent you from fully utilizing our website.

7. Your Rights Under the Privacy Act 2020

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Restriction: Request that we limit how we use your information
  • Objection: Object to our processing of your information in certain circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at enquiries@westtrades.co.nz

8. Marketing Communications

We may occasionally send you information about our services if:

  • You have explicitly consented to receive marketing communications
  • It relates directly to a project you have enquired about

You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email or contacting us directly.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (such as trade professional websites or social media). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies separately.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child, we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a revised date. Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us:

West Trades Group Ltd
Email: enquiries@westtrades.co.nz

13. Complaints

If you believe we have breached your privacy rights, please contact us first so we can address your concerns. If you are not satisfied with our response, you can make a complaint to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner:

Office of the Privacy Commissioner
Website: www.privacy.org.nz
Phone: 0800 803 909
