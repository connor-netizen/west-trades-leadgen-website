Requirements & Standards

Different projects have different requirements. Here's what we look for:

✓ Legal Operation

Registered as a sole trader or limited company. We work with legitimate businesses, not cash-job operators.

✓ Experience Level (Depends on Project Type)

Small jobs (maintenance, repairs, weekend work): Proven competency and quality work. Portfolio or references showing you can deliver.

Large projects (renovations, new builds, complex work): Minimum 3-5 years as an independent operator, full licensing, and comprehensive insurance. We need certainty you can deliver major work.

✓ Quality-Focused

You take pride in your work and stand behind it. Reputation matters more than rushing through jobs.

✓ Professional Communication

You respond to enquiries promptly, show up when you say you will, and communicate clearly with clients.

✓ Insurance (Project-Dependent)

Small jobs: Not always required but recommended.
Large projects: Public liability insurance mandatory. Full home renovations and complex work require proper coverage.

✓ Auckland-Based

Operating in Auckland and surrounding areas. We're focused on serving the local market well.

Common Questions

How much does it cost to join?

There's no upfront cost or monthly fees. We charge a small success fee only when you win a job through the network. No work = no cost.

What happens if a job grows into more work?

If a $5k deck job turns into $50k of ongoing work with that client, that's all yours. We're not taking ongoing fees on relationships you build. The client becomes your client—you can work with them directly from there. You'll probably still want to use our platform for the coordination benefits, but that's up to you.

How many jobs will I get?

It depends on your trade, location, availability, and how competitive your quotes are. We can't guarantee volume, but we match quality trades with quality projects.

Do I have to take every job offered?

No. You review project details and decide whether to quote. You're an independent operator—you choose what work you take.

Can I join if I'm currently working for someone else?

Yes. As long as you're legally set up as a sole trader, you're within your rights to take on your own work. Many trades use our network for weekend jobs while transitioning to full independence.

What if I'm already busy?

Perfect. We can set your availability status to "limited" or "future bookings." Join now, get set up, and activate when you're ready for more work.

Can I leave the network anytime?

Yes. No lock-in contracts. If it's not working for you, you can leave anytime. We want trades who want to be here.

Do you do in-person meetups?

Yes. We run regular catchups and events around West Auckland—usually at local cafes or spots in the community. It's a chance to meet other trades in the network, share knowledge, and build real relationships. When new trades join, we often have experienced network members there to meet them and provide input on fit. We're building a proper community, not just a digital platform.
